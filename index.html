Our story

The Centre for Innovation and Incubation, popularly known as Dr V.A.Sastry Innovation Centre is a G+2 building, with a floor space of approx. 30,000 sqft. The goal is to encourage entrepreneurial initiatives amongst the faculty and alumni of the NITW community in particular and to enrich the startup culture in state of Telangana. It gives an opportunity to explore and implement the innovative ideas into a commercially viable product. This centre facilitates interdisciplinary research with special emphasis on development and innovation of high-growth- knowledge-based-business and nurtures the indigenous products with innovative hardware/embedded designs. Innovation garage provides technical support, business mentoring and makerspace facility. Spreading across 30,000 sqft Innovation Garage has the adequate infrastructure for this endeavour.

Mission

Our mission is to be one of the leading incubator of India and offer a comprehensive ecosystem for mentoring, providing support and growth of start-ups with the aim of developing the nation as a hub of Entrepreneurship Research, Development and Training. We aim to ignite the young minds and motivate them in direction of enterpreneurship and startup culture. We aim to provide constant support to make their buisness ideas into a viable product. Through the events like Hack-a-thon we periodically capture ideas from students at initial stages and encourage them to go through the cycle of proving and demonstration, to develop business model and and finally register a start-up.

Incubated Startups

Epickbikes

Epick Bikes is an Innovation Garage incubated Hyderabad based Transportation technology startup that designs, develops and operates an unmanned rental system of E-Bikes.

Cusmat

Cusmat is a Virtual Reality startup founded by alumni of NIT Warangal which enables organizations to Onboard, Assess, Train, Upskill and Plan Learning Tracks for the employees to maximize retention rates and on the Job Performance by using their AR/VR, Cloud-based Learning Management system.

Kalam Notebooks

KALAM is a startup by students of NITW, that focuses on notebook advertising, a revolutionary step in the field of advertising. Their main aim is to reduce the cost of the notebook.

Gbit studios

A newly established indie game studio which develops professional games for mobile and PC platforms. GBit Studios consists of a group of individuals from National Institute of Technology Warangal who share a common passion and love for making games.

Technical Groups

Nevrónas is a machine Learning research group in National Institute of Technology, Warangal, India, which tries to spread and embrace Machine Learning research culture in the campus.

CybSecNITW is a group of information security enthusiasts from National Institute of Technology, Warangal which conduct and participate in various events across the country.

Events

Makerswave

Makerswave is an annual 3 days event filled with innovatiion challenges and events like makeathon, ACM codefest,Phantom CTF,etc to celebrate Maker culture,Innovation and entepreneurship and with a winning cash prize of more than 40k

Makeathon

A makeathon is a 24 hour event that aims to pull the most passionate students, faculty, entrepreneurs and industry experts for a weekend full of creativity and innovation.Students work with experienced mentors from industry and academia and try to build their idea into a viable product with clear buisness model.

Phantom CTF

A fun-filled contest organised by the CybSec team for trying to establish a foothold in Ethical Hacking and to increase awareness and curiousity in the domain of CyberSecurity

Workshops

Workshops,Seminars,Meets and Hackathons are organised on a regular Basis. NITW alumnis and other Successful individuals from different domains regularly visit Innovation Garage to have a talk with students ,to share their experience and to motivate them
